Lorentzian Length Spaces

Presented by Suhas B Mahesh — 1st Year PhD,

Wednesday, 08 April 2026 · 5:30 PM · Hall A317

Abstract

We try to define the Causality and Chronology structures on Lorentzian manifolds and further generalize them to length spaces. This is important in the theory of Relativity and the approaches to quantum gravity. We also define certain topologies on such spaces and see how these topologies affect the physics on such spaces. For all purposes in this seminar, we consider a Spacetime $(M,\bar{g})$, that is, a smooth/differentiable manifold $M$ with a Lorentzian metric $\bar{g}$ and a smooth, complete Riemannian metric $h$ on $M$.
