Lorentzian Length Spaces

Presented by Suhas B Mahesh — 1st Year PhD, Geometry and Topology

Wednesday, 1 April 2026  ·  5:30 PM  ·  Hall TBA

Abstract

We explore the spectral decomposition of compact self-adjoint operators $T: \mathcal{H} \to \mathcal{H}$ on a separable Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}$. The central result is the spectral theorem, which asserts that every compact self-adjoint operator admits an orthonormal basis of eigenvectors $\{\phi_n\}_{n=1}^\infty$ with corresponding eigenvalues $\lambda_n \to 0$. We will prove that $$T = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \lambda_n \langle \cdot, \phi_n \rangle \phi_n$$ and discuss applications to Fredholm integral equations of the second kind. Time permitting, we extend the discussion to non-self-adjoint compact operators via the singular value decomposition.
